About SAMSKRUTHI

SAMSKRUTHI is our flagship intercollegiate management and cultural fest that brings together talented students from across the country. This three-day extravaganza showcases the perfect blend of management acumen and cultural diversity.

From April 4th to 6th, 2025, participants will engage in various competitions, workshops, and cultural performances, making SAMSKRUTHI a platform for learning, networking, and celebrating talent. The fest embodies our commitment to holistic development and provides students with opportunities to showcase their skills beyond academics.

About BMS College of Commerce and Management

BMS College of Commerce and Management (BMSCCM) is a premier educational institution committed to excellence in commerce and management education. Established with a vision to nurture future business leaders, our college provides a dynamic learning environment that combines academic rigor with practical industry exposure.

Located in the heart of Bangalore, BMSCCM has been shaping careers and transforming lives through quality education, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities. Our curriculum is designed to meet the evolving demands of the business world while fostering innovation and entrepreneurial spirit among students.
